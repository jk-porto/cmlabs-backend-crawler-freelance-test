Lembaga Psikologi Terapan Universitas Indonesia (LPTUI) is an official institution under the Faculty of Psychology, University of Indonesia, with a longstanding track record in research, assessment, and human resource development. With decades of experience, LPTUI continues to innovate and adapt to technological advancements to support various national-scale assessment and HR development programs.

In mid-2025, LPTUI was entrusted to conduct a selection process for one of Indonesia’s government ministries, including administering psychometric assessments for more than 20,000 participants across the country.

To ensure an effective and digitally facilitated selection process, LPTUI partnered with cmlabs as its technology collaborator to provide the infrastructure for an integrated online psychotest system and selection portal. In this collaboration, cmlabs’ role focuses on developing and optimizing the digital systems that support the assessment operations—without intervening in or taking over the psychological selection and assessment processes, which remain fully managed by LPTUI.

Through a secure, scalable, and user-friendly technological approach, the cmlabs team designed and built a platform capable of handling thousands of participants simultaneously while maintaining data confidentiality and ensuring the integrity of the assessment process. Every element of the system was tailored to meet LPTUI’s scientific standards and professional ethics through close coordination and intensive discussions between both teams.

Get to Know LPTUI

Lembaga Psikologi Terapan Universitas Indonesia (LPTUI) is an official institution under the Faculty of Psychology, University of Indonesia, focusing on the application of psychological science across various fields, including education, industry, organizations, and communities.

Since its establishment, LPTUI has been recognized as one of Indonesia’s leading psychology institutions, known for its expertise, integrity, and significant contributions to human resource development.

Supported by a team of professional psychologists, researchers, and experienced consultants, LPTUI actively provides psychological assessment services, training programs, consultancy, and applied research.

cmlabs x LPTUI Collaboration in the Recruitment of a Government Ministry Selection Program

As an academic psychology institution with a national reputation, LPTUI plays a vital role in ensuring that every assessment process is conducted objectively, credibly, and in accordance with scientific standards.

To support the implementation of this large-scale testing initiative, LPTUI required a technology partner capable of delivering a secure, stable, and accessible digital platform that could accommodate thousands of participants simultaneously. This challenge demanded not only technical precision but also the seamless integration of psychometric principles with a robust digital infrastructure.

As part of this strategic collaboration, cmlabs, through its technology division cmlabs Orion, took on several key roles, including:

  • Developing a secure and stable online psychotest system.
  • Providing participant verification features tailored to the selection requirements.
  • Building a recruitment information portal and test access system.
  • Optimizing the user interface and experience to ensure accessibility for participants across Indonesia.

 

Through our subsidiary, cmlabs Orion, we played a strategic role in designing and developing a system that effectively addressed these challenges. With extensive experience in large-scale technology development and a strong focus on data security and accuracy, the cmlabs Orion team delivered a reliable, efficient, and scalable online psychotest platform to meet LPTUI’s operational needs.

The entire development process was carried out in close collaboration with the LPTUI team to ensure that every system component, from user interface design to participant data management, aligned with LPTUI’s psychometric principles and upheld the highest standards of professional ethics.

Website Development to Streamline the Selection Process

As part of our strategic partnership with LPTUI, cmlabs played a key role in developing a dedicated website designed to streamline the participant selection process for a major government program.

The website serves as a central hub that integrates every stage of the selection journey, from registration and verification to online psychometric testing and assessment result reporting, within one efficient and accessible system.

With a strong focus on user experience, the cmlabs development team created an intuitive and lightweight interface that ensures a smooth and accessible experience for participants from all backgrounds.

Building a Modern Digital Platform for Government Selection Programs

To support LPTUI in executing large-scale candidate assessments for one of Indonesia’s government ministries, cmlabs, through its technology subsidiary cmlabs Orion, developed a modern, secure, and fully integrated online psychotest platform.

The platform was purpose-built to handle extensive testing needs while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy, data security, and participant comfort. Every component was designed using a human-centered approach—from a clean, intuitive user interface to a robust backend that connects seamlessly in real time with LPTUI’s assessment database.

The system is adaptable to various types of psychological tests and evaluation methods. Leveraging Next.js-based monitoring and advanced encryption technologies, the platform ensures the entire testing process runs transparently, efficiently, and in full compliance with LPTUI’s psychometric standards.

cmlabs’ Commitment to Digital Transformation

Through this collaboration, cmlabs strengthens its commitment to supporting Indonesia’s ongoing digital transformation across strategic sectors. We believe that technology plays a vital role in advancing efficiency, transparency, and credibility, especially in areas such as psychological assessment and human resource development.

Looking ahead, cmlabs remains dedicated to creating impactful and sustainable digital solutions. We will continue to serve as a trusted partner for institutions, organizations, and government bodies, driving innovation that helps Indonesia move forward with confidence in the digital era.
